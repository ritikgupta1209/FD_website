A Reflection on Venturing Out from Mikah


This month,our founder,Jon Staff,invited Mikah Meyer ,travel writer and founder of Outside Safe Space, to share reflections on making nature more inclusive for the LGBTQ+ community.


As a tribute to his dad, Mikah visited all 400-plus sites in the National Park Service, and he wants to encourage everyone to travel - and be your authentic self.

Whether to complete a bucket list item, to visit a national park, or to spend time outdoors in a way that helps others feel welcome.Because no matter who we are, there's no time like the present to embrace ourselves and what we want to do with our lives.

MIKAH MEYER
